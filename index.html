Positioning

One engine. Six offers. One clear buyer path for each offer.

DBB2 is a basketball intelligence platform with six distinct products. Some are content-led, some are data-led, and some are decision-led. The site should make that split obvious immediately.

Six offers

The homepage should map directly to the six DBB2 products.

1. Team outcome

Basketball Operations

Decision support that helps teams win through better player and roster decisions.

  • Player evaluation, fit and role analysis, neutralized projections
  • Historical comparables, replay validation, custom reports
  • Primary buyer: basketball ops and front office

2. Team systems

Team Technical Integration

Feeds and schemas that plug DBB2 into internal team workflows.

  • API outputs, JSON/CSV feeds, dashboard inputs, automated pipelines
  • Custom schema mapping and workflow integration
  • Primary buyer: analytics engineering and technical leadership

3. Fastest route

Licensed Basketball Content

Ready-to-publish NBA analysis for publishers, newsletters, and fantasy media brands.

  • Articles, rankings, role-change notes, sleepers/busts, trade impact, rest-of-season analysis
  • White-label research and branded editorial packs
  • Primary buyer: editors and content leads

4. Product layer

Fantasy Product Data

Projections and valuation data for fantasy products and basketball apps.

  • Projection API, rankings API, comparable-player feeds
  • Risk and confidence layers, widgets, scheduled feeds
  • Primary buyer: product and engineering

5. Market pricing

Sportsbook Player-Prop Intelligence

Projections and research for prop pricing, trading, and sportsbook operations.

  • Player projections, fair value, volatility, confidence
  • Role and availability adjustments, prop research feeds
  • Primary buyer: trading, quant research, risk, product

6. Probability layer

Prediction-Market Intelligence

Basketball probability models and research for prediction platforms and adjacent products.

  • Player/team projections, probability models, market-supporting research
  • API or scheduled datasets, editorial feeds
  • Primary buyer: product, market ops, research, editorial
